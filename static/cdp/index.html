Customer Data Platform

The perfect platform to get all your data in one place, and deliver hyper personalized experiences to your customers.

First Things First: What is
Customer Data?

It could include data generated from online, offline, transactional, behavioral, sales, app, product usage, ad interactions, customer service and more. Before CDP's these were stored in separate databases, giving partial views of the customer. But with CDPs, data from all these disparate data sources makes its way into a centralized database, allowing you to understand the customer better, and letting all your companys departments from marketing to after-sales tap into this goldmine and perform at their best.

What is
Customer Data Platform

A Customer Data Platform is a software application that collects data from your customers (across multiple touchpoints) into a single, unified database. This data can then be used to give your customers a personalized, consistent, cross channel experience. Each further interaction contributes to the knowledge base, allowing you to keep refining your communication - helping you convert, retain, and cross-sell to your audience like never before. In the Customer Experience (CX) era, a CDP is your most important tool it gives you a complete picture of the customer. This 360-degree view allows you to hyper-personalize each of your customers interactions with you. Its like you know what your customer wants, when, and where theyre most likely to decide to buy.

WHY CDP?

CRM

The basic function of a CDP platform is to unify disparate data from multiple sources into a single, unique customer identity. This information can be added to and refined over the years, giving you a holistic, nuanced view of customer habits and likes.

Winning CX

CX is the final frontier for every business out there. Buying decisions are often based on how comfortable and loved your customers feel with you. And the only way to beat the competition in this game, is to know them better than anyone else. A CDP can tell you when to approach a customer, what to say, and where (the channel) theyre most conducive to buying.

Targeted Marketing

Easily create segments in your CDP database based on subtle or broad differences in your audience. Then create a set of actions for the software to take customers based on what your customers do, or dont. With a CDP, you can prepare for every twist and turn in the customer journey, helping them along to finally buying from you.

Data EqualityAcross Departments

CDP data can be used by all the departments in your organization, for just about any purpose. The data can be applied to business intelligence, reports, forecasting, marketing, service, sales and more. This ability to plug and play with CDP data means it can be your primary data source irrespective of how big your organization may be.

Adaptability

A CDP platform is also made to integrate seamlessly with multiple software and data sources. With the software as the fulcrum your marketing stack spins on, youre well placed to adapt to changing times, consumer behavior and technology shifts.

Operational & Organizational Efficiency

The quick-to-integrate nature of CDPs make it ready-to-go irrespective of what your marketing stack consists of today, or in the future. This gives you operational and organizational efficiency allowing you to speedily seize opportunities ahead of your competition.

Marketing Automation

Automating your marketing stack and operations is made easier with a CDP in your arsenal. You can segment your audience and disseminate your messaging across multiple channels, all based on real-time data.

Adhere to Privacy &Consent Regulations

Easily automate the consent and privacy process by giving the right options to the different segments of your audience. Allow them to make informed decisions and easily juggle priorities in a privacy-focussed future.
